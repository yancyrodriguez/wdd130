Overview

If there's one thing we know about Expeditions, it's rafting. These unique experience bring their own nuances offering adventures for beginners, intermediate, experienced and advanced paddlers. See and discover rugged natural beauty, rich history, dynamic culture and endless adventures.

Discover the joy of rafting along this mellow and scenic section of the National Park. The Upper New River Canyon is a fun-filled adventure perfect for families with young children and teens.
